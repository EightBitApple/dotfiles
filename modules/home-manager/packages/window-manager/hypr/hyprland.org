** Workspaces
Assign workspaces to outputs. Workspaces one to five will get assigned to the
primary monitor. Workspaces six to ten will get assigned to the secondary monitor.
#+begin_src nix
workspace = [
  "1, monitor:DP-1"
  "2, monitor:DP-1"
  "3, monitor:DP-1"
  "4, monitor:DP-1"
  "5, monitor:DP-1"
  "6, monitor:DP-2"
  "7, monitor:DP-2"
  "8, monitor:DP-2"
  "9, monitor:DP-2"
  "10, monitor:DP-2"
];
#+end_src
